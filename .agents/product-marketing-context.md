# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: May 9, 2026*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** AI-powered trading performance assistant for prop firm Forex day traders.
**What it does:** TradiaAI helps traders turn trade history into clear performance insights, risk feedback, behavior patterns, and AI-powered improvement plans.
**Product category:** AI Trading Journal and Trading Performance Intelligence Tool.
**Product type:** SaaS / Trading Tool.
**Business model:** Subscription-based (implied by "upgrade" and "pricing" routes).

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Prop firms (users are traders within these firms).
**Decision-makers:** Forex day traders.
**Primary use case:** Improving trading consistency and protecting funded accounts.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Analyze trading performance.
- Track risk behavior.
- Identify and reduce emotional mistakes.
- Pass prop firm challenges.
- Protect funded accounts.
**Use cases:**
- Landing page CRO.
- Homepage copywriting.
- Pricing page strategy.
- Onboarding flow improvement.
- SEO pages.
- Cold outreach.
- Launch strategy.
- Social content.
- Analytics and conversion tracking.
- Competitor comparison pages.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Traders lose funded accounts because of poor risk management and repeated emotional mistakes.
**Why alternatives fall short:** Most trading journals are too manual and boring. They don't provide fast feedback on what the trader is doing wrong.
**What it costs them:** Time, money (lost accounts/challenges), and emotional stress.
**Emotional tension:** Frustration from inconsistency, fear of losing funded status, doubt in their strategy.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Generic trading journals, manual spreadsheets.
**Secondary:** Performance coaching, automated risk management tools.
**Indirect:** Self-analysis, "hoping for the best."

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- AI-driven insights from trading data.
- Specific focus on prop firm Forex traders.
- Behavior pattern identification.
**How we do it differently:** Instead of just recording wins and losses, we analyze the *why* behind the performance.
**Why that's better:** Faster feedback loop and actionable improvement plans.
**Why customers choose us:** For serious traders who want a data-backed edge in prop firm trading.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Is it too manual? | TradiaAI automates the analysis to save time. |
| Does it work for my firm? | Designed specifically for prop firm Forex traders. |

**Anti-persona:** Casual/hobbyist traders not interested in deep performance analysis.

## Brand Voice
**Tone:** Clear, direct, serious, performance-focused, trader-native.
**Style:** Professional and data-driven.
**Personality:** Performance-focused, serious, non-hypey.

## Goals
**Business goal:** Growth in user base (prop firm traders) and conversion to paid plans.
**Conversion action:** Signup and upgrade to premium plans.
